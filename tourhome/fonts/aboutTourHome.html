so what is ToUrHome?

> ToUrHome is a website designed to match up users that plan on travelling and needing of an accommodation.

> For example, Raymond from Los Angeles has arranged to travel to Sydney from June to July and Jennifer from Sydney, Australia has arranged to travel to Los Angeles from November to December, ToUrHome will help facilitate a platform to help match them up. During each visit, Raymond and Jennifer will stay at their counterpart's residence.

> ToUrHome's goal is to match users from different corners of the world and provide them with an unforgettable stay.
